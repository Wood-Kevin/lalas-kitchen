Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 8, 2026

This privacy policy describes how Lala's Kitchen ("the app," "we," "us") handles information. Lala's Kitchen is a match-3 puzzle game developed by Kevin Wood.

Information We Collect Directly

Lala's Kitchen has no user accounts, no sign-up, and no server backend of its own. We do not collect your name, email address, or any other personally identifying information.

The app stores the following information locally on your device only, never transmitted to us or any server we control:

  • Game progress: completed levels, current level, star ratings earned
  • Recipe card collection progress
  • Lives count and regeneration timing
  • Your sound and haptics preferences
  • Which one-time tutorials you've already seen

This data lives entirely on your device (using your device's local storage) and is included in your device's own normal backup system if you have one enabled (such as iCloud Backup or Android's own backup service). We have no access to it, cannot see it, and cannot recover it if it's lost from your device.

Crash and Error Logging

If the app encounters an unexpected error, a basic log of that error is kept locally on your device to help with troubleshooting. This log is not automatically transmitted anywhere and stays on your device.

Advertising

Lala's Kitchen offers optional rewarded video ads that you can choose to watch in exchange for in-game benefits (extra moves or a lives refill). These ads are never required to play or progress through the game.

When you choose to watch a rewarded ad, that ad is served through Google AdMob. This third-party ad provider may collect information such as your device's advertising identifier, general device information, and approximate location, in order to serve and measure ads. This collection is governed by their own privacy policies, not ours:

  • Google AdMob: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google's advertising partners: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

You can review and adjust your ad personalization settings, or reset your device's advertising identifier, through your device's own privacy settings (iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking / Apple Advertising; Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads).

Children's Privacy

Lala's Kitchen is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us using the information below and we will address it.

Your Choices

Since all gameplay data stays on your device, you can delete it entirely at any time by uninstalling the app, or by clearing the app's storage through your device's own settings. Rewarded ads are entirely optional and never required to play.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same address with a new effective date above.
